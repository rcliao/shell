### B-009 — heartbeat noop investigation ✅ COMPLETE (resulted in B-010)
The premise was wrong. Noop IS firing — we just measured it badly.

**What actually happens** (`internal/bridge/bridge.go:852–882`):
1. Claude emits `[noop]`.
2. `stripDirectives()` removes the marker (`legacyDirectiveRe` at `artifacts.go:18` includes `noop`).
3. `response` becomes `""`.
4. Line 873 has `response != ""` guard before `RecordTranscript` — empty responses are correctly excluded from shared transcript.
5. Line 868 still logs the empty response to per-agent `shell.db` (internal session store).

**Why our cycle-0 metric showed 0 noops:** we queried `shared/transcript-v2.db` for `text LIKE '%[noop]%'`. By the time text reaches that DB, `[noop]` has been stripped. The query is structurally incapable of finding noops.

**Real measurement should:** count empty-string assistant rows in `~/.shell/agents/<n>/shell.db`, OR add a counter at the strip site.
